Get To Know Timo

I do, however, enjoy building something from the ground up which, fortunately, programming allows me to do since a programmer can technically build whatever they want, given the right skillset and an adequate timeframe.

  • Biology
  • Neuroscience
  • Psychology
  • Space exploration
  • History
  • Philosophy
  • Artificial Intelligence

I chose ICT because out of all of these I believe being a programmer is going to give me the best chance at having the most amount of free time to explore whatever domain I want.
